Our Mission

SXSCLI Awards was created to provide the technology community with transparent, independent, and comprehensive rankings of the tools and technologies that power modern software development.

In a world of rapidly evolving technology, developers and organizations need reliable, unbiased information to make informed decisions. Our rankings combine rigorous benchmarking, expert evaluation, and community feedback to deliver the most accurate technology assessments available.

We cover the full spectrum of developer technology — from AI models and coding assistants to programming languages and frameworks — providing a holistic view of the technology landscape.
